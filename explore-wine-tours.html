Our expertly crafted wine tour packages to the top wine regions in France.

Whether you are looking for a complete luxury tour in one of France's best wine regions, or you are simply looking to book a hotel in Bordeaux, we have the best recommendations for you here.

Burgundy

Our Burgundy package offers the complete Burgundy experience, giving you the opportunity to discover the main appellations of the region. You will explore the Pays Chablisien, the Côte de Nuits, the Côte de Beaune, the Côte Chalonnaise and the Côte Mâconnaise. You will sample some delicious local food and visit some charming villages and the historic town of Beaune.

Bordeaux

Our Bordeaux package offers the complete Bordeaux experience, giving you the opportunity to discover the main appellations of the region – Médoc, Saint-Emilion and Pomerol – sample some delicious local food and explore the UNESCO World Heritage City. You will learn particularly about the Cabernet Sauvignon grape and gaining an insight into what makes those big, bold reds so renowned.

Champagne

On this Extensive Champagne package, you will have the opportunity to explore the Champagne vineyards and taste Champagne from different appellations, from the Montagne de Reims to the Côtes des Bar, going through the Vallée de la Marne, the Côtes des Blancs and the Côte de Sézanne. You will also explore Reims, Epernay and Troyes, and sample some tasty local dishes.
