Burgundy

Our Extensive Burgundy package offers the complete Burgundy experience, giving you the opportunity to discover the main appellations of the region. You will explore the Pays Chablisien, the Côte de Nuits, the Côte de Beaune, the Côte Chalonnaise and the Côte Mâconnaise. You will sample some delicious local food and visit some charming villages and the historic town of Beaune. Your trip will start in the quaint village of Chablis where you will learn all about these popular white wines. Upon arrival, check into your Comfort room at the 4* Hostellerie des Clos. In the afternoon, take a short walk to the cellars of a premium producer of Chablis wines for a tasting session of four of their wines. In the evening, a three course dinner with wines, water and coffee will be served in the hotel’s restaurant.

Bordeaux

Our Extensive Bordeaux package offers the complete Bordeaux experience, giving you the opportunity to discover the main appellations of the region – Médoc, Saint-Emilion and Pomerol, Graves, Sauternes – sample some delicious local food and explore the UNESCO World Heritage City. Upon arrival in Bordeaux, check into your Classic room at the 4* Hotel de Seze, perfectly located in the city centre, just a few minutes away from the restaurants, shops and bars. Your trip will start with a tutored tasting session of five French red wines (including three Bordeaux wines), in a quaint wine bar, located within walking distance of your hotel. You will visit three Châteaux, learning particularly about the Cabernet Sauvignon grape and gaining an insight into what makes those big, bold reds so renowned.

Champagne

Our Extensive range is designed to give you the opportunity to explore a wine region in its entirety. On this Extensive Champagne package, you will have the opportunity to explore the Champagne vineyards and taste Champagne from different appellations, from the Montagne de Reims to the Côtes des Bar, going through the Vallée de la Marne, the Côtes des Blancs and the Côte de Sézanne. You will also explore Reims, Epernay and Troyes, and sample some tasty local dishes. After checking in your Tradition room at the 4* Hotel de la Paix on the first day, you will be welcomed at a tasting bar owned by a local Champagne House in the city centre. Here, enjoy a fun and insightful introduction to Champagne, with three tastings served alongside a cheese or charcuterie platter.
