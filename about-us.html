About Us

A family owned and operated luxury wine tour company.

Pinot Gogo is a Luxury Travel Specialist, operating since 2021 and offering exclusive Mercedes chauffeured tours in Champagne, Bordeaux, and Burgundy. We specialize in luxurious custom designed vacations, events and incentives related to food and wine. We are proud members of ASTA (American Society of Travel Agents), Slow Food, UNAV (Travel Agency Association in Spain), and the IACP (International Association of Culinary Professionals).
